System: Trading Back to Fair Value Without Stops
Philosophy: As long as you pick an instrument trading at an extreme, a trade back to fair value without stoploss is a high probabolity trade.
Timeframe: Daily
Instrument: @AD
Long/Short: Long only
Entry:
  1- Price closes below the lower Bollinger Band
  2- Price then closes back above the lower Bollinger Band( 130 length, 1.1 stdev)
Exit: Enter using same entry up to 3 times and close all trades when profit reaches 1,250
Stop Loss: none
